Chili

Description

This is a delicious chili recipe

Ingredients

  • Sausage, spicy italian
  • Whole peeled tomatoes, 28oz can
  • Kidney beans, 12oz can

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage
  2. Add the tomatoes
  3. Simmer 30 minutes
  4. Add beans and return to simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Serve with sour cream and other toppings
